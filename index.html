About Me

My name is Andrew Rice. I am 30 years old and was born in Little Rock, Arkansas. I moved a great deal while I was growing having moved 11 times and living 9 different states. Because of this moving I was lucky enough to have visited the entirety of the continental United States. But that's enough about where I've been, now onto more about myself!!!

I am an avid listener of music and I would say it is one of the most important things in my life. I listen to all sorts of different music and get a lot of inspiration for music from local indepedent radio station KEXP where I am also a volunteer. I love music so much that some friends and I started up a tape label. The tape label is called Den Tapes and puts out exclusively local Seattle bands. Many of them are punk bands but we have also put out others.

Look forward to meeting and working with everyone in class!!!
